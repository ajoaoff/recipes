Ingredients:
 - 4 sausages
 - 4 hot dog breads
 - 1 can tomato sauce
 - ketchup, mostard and mayonnaise

Directions:
 1. Boil the sausages.
 2. Put the tomato sauce in a pan and warm it up.
 3. Put the sausages in the pan with the sauce.
 4. Open the breads, put one sausage with tomato sauce
    and use ketchup, mostard and mayonnaise as you wish.
